BIO

"Like a twisted, feminine Little Nemo for our current generation, Damascan Daydreams transports us across a collective subconscious of exotic, electronic Pop-inspired sounds." -Jack Anderson, KUTX Austin

photo by Candice Ghai Photography

Damascan Daydreams is Eman Tiba, a singer/songwriter who crafts dreamy dark pop with electronic undertones. Born in Damascus, Syria, and raised in Dublin, Ohio, her songwriting started at a very young age singing in a school choir while playing keyboard and tabla. Currently residing in Austin, Texas, Damascan Daydreams released her debut, "Haunted Home" in 2021 and then released her EP "Hidden World" in 2023.

Shortly after, the EP "Archangel" was released in August 2024 featuring Oddmanrush. A new collaborative single release with Joseph Salazar is scheduled to be released on September 19th 2025.
