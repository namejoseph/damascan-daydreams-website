BIO

DAMASCAN DAYDREAMS IS A MUSICAL PROJECT EXPLORING THE BOUNDARIES BETWEEN ATMOSPHERIC DREAM POP, ELECTRONIC, AND AMBIENT GENRES. WITH ETHEREAL SOUNDSCAPES AND EMOTIVE VOCALS, THE PROJECT WEAVES NARRATIVES THAT TRANSPORT LISTENERS TO OTHERWORLDLY REALMS.

DRAWING INSPIRATION FROM BOTH NATURAL LANDSCAPES AND INNER EMOTIONAL TERRAINS, DAMASCAN DAYDREAMS CREATES SONIC EXPERIENCES THAT INVITE REFLECTION AND CONNECTION WITH THE DEEPER ASPECTS OF HUMAN EXPERIENCE.

THE PROJECT HAS RELEASED SEVERAL SINGLES AND EPS SINCE ITS INCEPTION, INCLUDING THE RECENT WORKS "FROM THE HEAVENS" AND "HIDDEN WORLD EP."
